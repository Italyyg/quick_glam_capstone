About Quick Glam

We are a press on nail company founded by France Mendoza in 2018. France was always in love with coloring her nails as a little girl, she would go to the dollar store and buy plain glue on nails and would decorate them. Growing up she saw that press on nails didnt have a variety of selection and it would force someone to go to the nail salon. Her and our goal is to make it easier for everyone to have a set of fresh nails in a matter of seconds. We know the nail salon can be expensive and time consuming. Sometimes you get tired of the same set you have on, and are looking for a new fresh pair but dont want to go back to the salon, or maybe your nails dont match your outfit. We provide a solution with our variety of styles and lenghts to choose from!
